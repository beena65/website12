Expertise

DV Excellus brings years of experience from a team of passionate and dedicated young minds working in the agriculture and food sector of Nepal. Operated from three market centers i.e. Pokhara, Kathmandu and Chitwan of Nepal, and having presence in more than 10 districts, our Kheti platform is connecting and building relationships between thousands of farmers and consumers both digitally and physically. It is the high-time to redefine the food systems of Nepal.

Our Product

KHETI: Consciously Farming, Consuming, an agri-food tech digital platform, is a flagship product of DV Excellus. It is an integrated web and mobile-based platform with three modules:KHETI Farm,Farm Management Tool, KHETI Food.

Kheti integrates the entire value chain in food production and consumption, primarily delivering services to two major actors in the food systems– FARMERS and CONSUMERS, while other agri-ecosystem players such as cooperatives, development agencies, governments, products/service providers etc. can use the platform to interact and engage with the major actors.

We serve

Smallholder-Farmers and Consumers are the key players in the agri-food ecosystem yet they are the most vulnerable ones given the consequences of global issues such as climate change to health & nutrition. To transform the current food systems into more sustainable food systems it is an urgency to render solutions that benefit and give power to these major actors in the food systems. And, this is exactly where our keen focus is.

About Us

Welcome to Dv Excellus

DV EXCELLUS is a tech company formed by the merger between an agri-tech project spin-off from Development Voyage Pvt. Ltd. and EXCELLUS Solutions established in 2016.

Carrying over the legacy of Development Voyage working in agriculture and food sectors for more than a decade, and expertise of EXCELLUS technological interventions in agronomy, DV EXCELLUS envisions to design, develop and implement innovative solutions in the sectors of food and agriculture. After 3 years of R&D and piloting of the agri-food-tech platform concept, KHETI platform was commercially launched in January 2019.

Projects &collaboration

our projects

DV Excellus collaborates and partners with different companies, organizations - government and non-government, financial institutions to escalate its mission.

DV Excellus is collaborating with the Australian Embassy in Nepal (DFAT), Palladium and Upaya City Cargo in the BPP project. This project will help Kheti platform to reach 25,000 smallholder farmers in 15 districts and 250 plus B2B buyers in 4 market centers of Nepal. The project will run from July 2021 to December 2023.

Under the project collaboration with Upaya City Cargo, DV Excellus is piloting a digital literacy training for 600 farmers of Kaski, Kathmandu and Chitwan under SEEP Project. This one year project will run until February 2022.

SEEP

DV Excellus has entered into a MOU with Nabil Bank to digitalize agri-loan for smallholder farmers. Jointly, we will create a digital system where farmers can access collateral free loans of upto NPR 200,000 at their fingertips through Kheti platform.
